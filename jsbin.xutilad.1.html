MY FAVOURITE FOOD

My favourite food is jolof rice.

The following are the ingredients required to prepare Jolof Rice:

  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Tomato Paste
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Water
  • Seasoning
  • Onions
  • Meat
To prepare Jolof Rice follow the following steps:
  1. Wash the meat and put it in a pot after cutting it to piece
  2. Add some onions
  3. Add salt about 2 teaspoons
  4. Add water
  5. Put on fire for about 15 minutes
  6. Add oil and tomato paste
  7. Add the seasoning and pepper
  8. Leave it for 10 minutes
  9. Add water
  10. Allow it to boil
  11. Add your rice
  12. Allow it to cook for 30 minutes
